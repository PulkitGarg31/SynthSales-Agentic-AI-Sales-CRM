AI-Powered B2B Outreach & Lead Generation
Platform

Product Requirement & System Workflow Document
(PRD)

1. Product Overview

Objective

Build an AI-powered outreach and lead generation platform that automates:
      Company research
      Lead qualification
      Contact discovery
      Email verification
      Personalized outreach
      Follow-up automation
      Conversation tracking
      Meeting scheduling
      Customer notifications

The platform should operate as an end-to-end intelligent sales outreach assistant while still
allowing user review and control.

2. User Inputs

The platform will collect the following inputs from customers:

Company Target Data

      CSV file containing target companies

Product Information
      Product/service details
      Value proposition
      Industry category
      Key differentiators

Target Customer Requirements

      Ideal customer profile
      Industry preference
      Geography preference (optional)
      Company size preference
      Business requirements
      Customer-defined ranking criteria

Outreach Configuration

      Email template/format (Optional)
      Email footer/signature
      Tone preference
      Outreach personalization level

3. Backend Workflow

Phase 1: Company Intake & Research

Objectives

Process uploaded company data and gather intelligence.

Workflow

Company Processing

      Parse uploaded CSV
      Validate company data
      Remove duplicates
      Normalize company information
Automated Company Research

AI will collect:

      Company profile
      Industry
      Products/services
      Business model
      Company size
      Public information
      Website insights
      Market relevance
      Recent Funding
      Recent Negative News
      Active Hiring or not (Active Hiring is a positive sign)

Phase 2: Company Scoring & Ranking

Objective

Rank companies based on customer requirements.

Weighted Scoring Parameters

Possible factors:

      Product fit
      Industry alignment
      Company relevance
      Requirement satisfaction
      Market compatibility
      Growth indicators

Workflow

Platform will:

  1. Assign weighted score
  2. Generate explainable scoring
  3. Rank all companies
  4. Select top N companies specified by customer
Phase 3: Contact Discovery

Objective

Identify relevant decision-makers.

Workflow

For each shortlisted company:

Contact Search

Find:
      Top 3 sales contacts
      Decision makers
      Relevant business personnel

Contact Information

Collect:
      Name
      Role
      Linked profile (if available)
      Company association

Phase 4: Email Discovery & Verification

Objective

Generate and verify email addresses.

Workflow

Email Guessing Logic

Generate emails using:
      Company domain
      Standard patterns
      Name-domain combinations
Examples:
      firstname@company.com
      firstname.lastname@company.com
      f.lastname@company.com

Email Verification

Use Verifalia.
Logic:

      Verify guessed emails
      Stop guessing once verified email found
      Mark verification status
Possible states:
      Verified
      Risky
      Invalid
      Unknown

Phase 5: AI Outreach Generation

Objective

Create personalized outreach. Use AI API key

Email Generation Inputs

      Customer template (optional)
      Customer product details
      Company research
      Contact role
      Email footer

Workflow

Platform will:
      Personalize outreach
      Generate subject line
      Generate body content
      Maintain tone consistency
      Create recipient-specific messaging

Phase 6: Automated Outreach Execution

Objective

Send emails.

Workflow

      Use verified email only
      Send outreach
      Track delivery status
      Store thread history
Possible states:
      Queued
      Sent
      Delivered
      Failed

Phase 7: Email Monitoring & Follow-Up
Automation

Objective

Automate conversation management.

Tracking Frequency

Every 15 minutes.
Workflow

System will:
      Monitor inbox
      Detect unread/unanswered emails
      Analyze prior thread
      Generate contextual follow-up
      Send intelligent replies

Continue until:
      Meeting scheduled
      Campaign stopped
      Lead closed

Phase 8: Meeting Coordination

Objective

Manage meetings.

Workflow

When meeting fixed:
Platform will:

      Capture meeting link
      Store meeting details
      Notify customer
      Notify company contact
      Maintain meeting history
